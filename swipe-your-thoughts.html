Choose your Thoughts


It's easy to get caught up in negative thoughts. Let's practice POSITIVE and INSTRUCTIVE thoughts.



Positive thoughts are motivational.
  • You've got this.
  • You can do it.

    • Positive thoughts are optimistic.
  • It will be fine.
  • No problemo.

    • POSITIVE THOUGHTS

    You are pretty bad at life!
    You're great!
    No big deal!
    I've seen stewed apricots with prettier faces than yours.
    You suck!
    You're doing fine.
    You'll bounce back.
    You're basically a sausage!
    You'll be alright.
    You're a total loser!
    All good. No big deal.

    Is this a
    POSITIVE thought?


    Focus on what you should do. Not what you shouldn't do.
  • I should work on my catching.

    • Instead of

  • Stop dropping the ball.

    • Instructive thoughts are actionable.
  • I just need to keep training.
  • I need to work harder.
  • I just need to try again.

    • INSTRUCTIVE THOUGHTS

    You've got a long way to go.
    You're great!
    Time for a sausage-roll break.
    I'll give this another go.
    You're a proper bollard!
    Give it another go.
    You suck!
    Keep working at it.
    Maybe try NOT sucking.
    You'll never get there.
    Gotta keep training!

    Is this an
    INSTRUCTIVE thought?

    REMEMBER

    Choose your Thoughts

    Don't beat yourself up with negative thoughts.

    Choose thoughts that will help you bounce back.

    POSITIVE THOUGHTS

    "No big deal."

    "You'll get through this."

    "You've got this."

    INSTRUCTIVE THOUGHTS

    "Just need to train some more."

    "I need to work harder."
